About the Production

Andrew Lloyd Webber’s *The Phantom of the Opera* is a landmark Broadway musical that blends romance, mystery, and operatic music. It became the longest-running show in Broadway history and is known for its iconic chandelier scene and dramatic score.

Key Elements of the Show

  1. Music & Score
    • Composed by Andrew Lloyd Webber
    • “The Music of the Night”
    • Orchestral + operatic fusion
  2. Stage Design
    • Chandelier drop scene
    • Underground lair beneath the opera house
  3. Legacy
    • Longest-running Broadway show
    • Global cultural phenomenon
